Home

Hi! I'm Jacob and this is my projects website. On this site, I have created a variety of pages which list some of my hobbies, and those pages provide both examples of what I've done with those hobbies and some instructions to get started with them yourself. Gamedev

Gamedev, short for game development, is the process of making video games.
